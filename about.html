About me

My Story

Early on I knew my passions lie within psychology as well as being creative. Now there are a few avenues in life one can decide upon in order to work within these areas. The one I chose was marketing. Not only studying marketing management but working in the field for a few years, I realized that marketing was a very interesting and extremely diverse field – just not for me. I wanted to do more good other than just selling people stuff they may or may not need. On the search for something which would allow me to do just this in a creative manner, while utilizing the skills I acquired up until then, I stumbled on UI design. Diving headfirst into a career change course while working part-time in a small company which sought after someone to do a relaunch was not only perfect to apply learned things and solidify them, but also one of the best ways one could spend time during a world-wide pandemic. I believe there is always a new, better way to do things which is why I love to do things differently and try stuff out. In my experience, trying out is the best way to explore great things. After all, trying out UI design led me to discovering my true calling. When I am not designing, you’ll probably find me reading, doing sports outside or playing videogames with my friends. I am also in love with coffee and gin, so you’re bound to have a great drink no matter when we are going to meet!
